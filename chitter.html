Chitter

Details

Role: Designer
When: July 2016
Tools: Adobe Illustrator · Photoshop · PaintTool SAI

Background

A personal project, I wanted to create a poster for the app Chitter (now called Chillabit) in the style of Game of Thrones house banners. This was inspired by a post on there where a user generated a Game of Thrones banner template; so credits to him for the concept.

Challenges

The biggest challenge was to emulate not only the Game of Thrones house banners but real life animal heraldry as well. I searched examples of not only squirrels but other animals as well so I could emulate heraldry characteristics to be integrated into the squirrel; this made the squirrel not only look regal but intimidating as well.

Insights

Despite being a personal project done on my own time, it taught me about time management and the finish of a product. I did this project while attending school, and set deadlines on what was to be completed by a certain time.

As for the reception to the product, it got 400+ “likes” on the app where I posted it. I also got messages from friends and strangers complimenting it and wanting to use it as a wallpaper on their personal devices.
