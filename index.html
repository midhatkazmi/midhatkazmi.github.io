I graduated with a Bachelor's in the School of Interactive Arts and Technology with a specialization in Design at Simon Fraser University.

Originally more of an artist, I started getting into design when I was asked to create a jersey for my high school team, eventually landing on user interface design. But I wanted to do more than design mockups and prototypes; I wanted to implement the functionality. This lead me to web development, where I continue my journey into frontend and fullstack development and beyond.

Outside the code-o-sphere, I can be found drawing while listening to mystery podcasts, playing old video games I couldn't beat as a kid or expanding my vocabulary in my native language.
